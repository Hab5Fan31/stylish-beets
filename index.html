55 to 65 days are needed from germinationto harvest of the root.

The usually deep purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled, or roastedas a cooked vegetable, cold as a saladafter cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable.
